Dark Bronze 5005 anodized aluminum sheet  is visually attractive, extremely corrosion resistant, lightweight, and durable. The colored anodized layer means that the color is part of the metal, and so will not chip, flake, or peel like paint, it is also non-toxic and doesn’t contain any harmful byproducts. 5005 Anodized sheets are typically used in structural/architectural applications, appliances, food preparation equipment, furniture, sporting goods, boats, motor vehicles, jewelry, and artwork.

The advantage of Anodized Aluminum Sheet

1. Good processability
Anodized Aluminum Sheet is decorative strong, moderate hardness, it can bend easily, suitable for continuous high-speed stamping, convenient to process into products directly, no need complicated surface treatment, greatly reduce the production cycle and production cost.
2. Good weathering resistance
The standard anodizing layer 3μm of Anodized Aluminum Sheett for the indoor long time use, is not discolored, non-corroding, not oxidated and no rust. With thick anodized layer 10μm of anodized aluminum sheet, suitable for outdoor use, it is not discolored during expose to the sunlight.

After anodizing, the aluminum surface become high hardness, up to gem grade, good scratch resistance. There is no paint cover, preserve the metallic color of the aluminum sheet, highling the modern metallic feel, improve product level and added value.
